ONLINE MEETING HELP

Normally, the TSSG Wednesday general meeting is held at a library; usually at the Acton Memorial library. Due to the coronavirus prevention rules currently in place, TSSG has shifted its meetings to online only. Information on this page is intended to help with current online meeting connections.
 

Technical Skills Share Group Meetings

Due to library closings we are online only.

Anyone with an interest in any aspect of the Software Development Lifecycle is welcome to attend to see if this group is of interest to you.

Besides developers this might include web designers, project managers, support persons, or those interested in learning about these roles, but others are welcome too. If you are not sure where you may fit in, we will help you to figure it out.

When: 12:30 pm to 4:00 pm, every Wednesday, unless otherwise posted.
Where: Online only, use zoom* while libraries are closed.

Usual Times            Agenda
12:00 to 12:30 pm Set-up
12:30 to 1:00 pm Intros, Announcements and New Member Introductions
1:00 to 1:50 pm Web Application Project (PHP, JavaScript, HTML5)
1:50 to 2:00 pm Break
2:00 to 2:50 pm Mobile Application Project (Java, Android)
2:50 to 3:00 pm Break
3:00 to 3:50 pm Data Analytics Group (Python, R, Hadoop)
3:50 to 4:00 pm Plan agenda for next meeting; clean up
